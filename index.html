About PSBS

Pabitra Secondary Boarding School

Established in 2040 B.S., PSBS is committed to providing global standard education in a disciplined, supportive, and student-centered environment.

We nurture every learner to achieve academic excellence, confidence, and strong moral characters with modern digital integrations.
